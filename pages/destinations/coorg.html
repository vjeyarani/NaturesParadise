Coorg

Misty hills, coffee estates & peaceful nature escapes.

Why Visit Coorg?

Known as the Scotland of India, Coorg is filled with lush hills, cool misty mornings, coffee plantations, waterfalls, and calm villages — perfect for families seeking a quiet nature break.

Top Experiences

Coffee Estate Walks

Misty trails through lush coffee and spice plantations.

Abbey Waterfalls

Gentle cascades surrounded by thick green forests.

Hilltop Viewpoints

Soft sunrise moments with panoramic mountain views.

Family Travel Tips

1. Early mornings are the most scenic — plan short walks.
2. Carry light jackets — Coorg stays cool and misty.
3. Choose stays inside estates for peaceful nature time.
4. Avoid late-night drives — fog builds quickly.

Where to Stay
