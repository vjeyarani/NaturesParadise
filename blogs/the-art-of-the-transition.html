The Art of the Transition

Why the journey matters more than the destination.

Most travellers focus on the hotel. They obsess over the room view. But they forget the four hours before they get there.

There is nothing luxurious about dragging a suitcase over Venetian cobblestones because you didn't know the water taxi stops 500 meters away from the hotel entrance.
At XPLORE world, we obsess over the 'Transitions.'

We know which train platform in Florence has the elevator (so you don't carry bags up stairs).

We know which airport transfer service tracks your flight delay automatically.

We know that arriving in Positano after sunset means missing the most beautiful drive in Europe.

Great travel design is invisible. You only notice it when it goes wrong. We make sure you never notice it."
